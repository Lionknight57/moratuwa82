Fifty years since the 1977 intake. The organizing committee has settled the venue
and the shape of the weekend: **two nights and three days at Centauria Lake
Resort, Udawalawe**, travelling together by bus from UoM on Saturday 12 June and
returning on Monday 14 June. We cut the anniversary cake on **Sunday 13 June 2027**.

As of the 27 June 2026 circular, **115 participants** are expected and
**Rs 2,316,400** has been confirmed in sponsorships — which brings the cost down
to roughly **Rs 19,000 per person** sharing a double room.

[**See the full itinerary and cost breakdown →**](/reunion-2027/)

A big thank you to everyone who has sponsored so far. Account details for
sponsorship payments will be shared first; all other payments will be collected
three months before the reunion.
